Our Heritage

Founded in Paris in 1924 by master jeweler Antoine Lumière, our maison has been at the forefront of haute joaillerie for nearly a century. What began as a small atelier in Place Vendôme has grown into an internationally renowned house of luxury.

Through wars, economic shifts, and changing fashions, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and uncompromising quality. Each piece that leaves our workshop carries with it nearly a century of tradition and expertise.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Every LUMIÈRE piece is handcrafted in our Paris atelier by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to the art of fine jewelry. Our craftsmen undergo rigorous training, often spending decades perfecting their skills.

We use only the finest materials—ethically sourced gemstones, conflict-free diamonds, and recycled precious metals. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that beauty never comes at the cost of our planet.

From the initial sketch to the final polish, each piece undergoes over 300 hours of meticulous work, ensuring that every detail meets our exacting standards.
